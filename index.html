Yu-Gi-Oh! FM Database

Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories is a video game from Playstation, with a mechanic called "Fusions", this one allow the player to fuse cards together to get a new, hopefully more powerful card.

For this reason we made this project, to discover the possibilities to fusion your cards!
